About Me

I am Ph.D student at Laboratoire d'Informatique de Modélisation et d'Optimisation des Systèmes (LIMOS) at Université Clermont-Auvergne in Network and Security departement. My current research is focused on distributed secure computations, where several agents communicate in order to produce a result in a secure way. My Ph.D Supervisor is Pascal Lafourcade who is HDR Lecturer in Security.
