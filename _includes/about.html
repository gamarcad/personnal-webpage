About Me

I am Ph.D student at Laboratoire d'Informatique de Modélisation et d'Optimisation des Systèmes (LIMOS) at Université Clermont-Auvergne in Network and Security departement. My current research is focused on distributed secure computations, where several agents communicate in order to produce a result without learn additional data.

My Ph.D Supervisor is Pascal Lafourcade working at LIMOS and my Co-Supervisors are Radu Ciucanu and Marta Soare, working at Laboratoire d'Informatique de Grenoble (LIG).
